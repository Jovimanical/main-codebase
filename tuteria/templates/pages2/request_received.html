What happens next?

First, we search

We'll check our records of thousands of verified tutors to find those that suit your needs. If we do, we'll contact you in 30mins - 2hrs. If not, we'll instruct our quality team to look for tutors in your area and run them through our assessment and verification process to ensure they are top-notch. This may take up to 2 - 4 days, but we'll try to be as fast as possible without compromising on quality or safety.

Then contact you

We'll send you an email and SMS containing links to two or three qualified tutors who meet your requirements so that you can review them too. If you don't actively check your emails or SMS, that's fine. One of our consultants, most likely Godwin, will give you a call.

You evaluate tutors

Compare their experiences and styles of teaching to find out who is right for you. You can learn about their education, where they have worked and what other clients have said about them. This helps you get to know a bit about your tutor before deciding to book lessons.

And book lessons!

When you've chosen your preferred tutors, it's easy to hire them! Just use their real-time calendar to select the days and times you want lessons to hold, then complete your booking. Your tutors will contact you shortly afterwards to plan and prepare for lessons.
