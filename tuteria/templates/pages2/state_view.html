{% if request.is_featured %}How we select our home tutors {% else %}How we select our home tutors{% endif %}

A great tutor makes all the difference in the life of a child. While we are eager to recruit tutors in all subjects, we maintain very strict selection criteria in order to protect our high standards. In time past, we've helped more than 2,105 students prepare for exams.

  • 1. High Standards for Quality
    For a start, tutors must have extensive knowledge of each subject they wish to teach. E.g. a Math tutor will be a Math guru who can simplify difficult topics, build confidence in your child and turn their C's to A's.

  • 2. Teaching Ability
    Not every smart person can teach. We go the extra mile to look for teaching ability, superb communication and a friendly personality.

  • 3. A heart for Mentoring
    The biggest factor we consider is whether a candidate is serious about being a role model, a mentor and an encourager in the lives of our students. All tutors must pass this assessment.
