Flexible

Flexible: 6 hours notice required for full refund

  • If the client cancels a single lesson less than 6 hours before it commences, the cost of the first hour is non-refundable but remaining hours are 100% refunded.
  • If a booking contains more than one session and the client cancels the entire booking before it starts, 10% of the booking cost is paid to tutor, while 90% is refunded to the client.
  • A lesson is officially canceled when the client clicks the 'Cancel' button on the cancellation confirmation page.

Moderate

Moderate: 12 hours notice required for full refund

  • If the client cancels a single lesson less than 12 hours before it commences, the cost of the first hour is non-refundable but remaining hours are 90% refunded.
  • If a booking contains more than one session and the client cancels the entire booking before it starts, 15% of the booking cost is paid to tutor, while 85% is refunded to the client.
  • A lesson is officially canceled when the client clicks the 'Cancel' button on the cancellation confirmation page.

Strict

Strict: 24 hours notice required for full refund

  • If the client cancels a single lesson less than 24 hours before it commences, the cost of the first hour is non-refundable but remaining hours are 80% refunded.
  • If a booking contains more than one session and the client cancels the entire booking before it starts, 20% of the booking cost is paid to tutor, while 80% is refunded to the client.
  • A lesson is officially canceled when the client clicks the 'Cancel' button on the cancellation confirmation page.

Long Term

Long Term: 30% down payment required for all bookings

  • Note: this policy automatically applies to all bookings of 24 hours or more.
  • If the client books a long term lesson and decides to cancel the long term agreement before the start date, 30% of payment is credited to the tutor while 70% is refunded.
  • If the client books a long term lesson and decides to cancel the long term agreement or any session during the lesson, 30% of payment is credited to the tutor while 70% is refunded to client.
